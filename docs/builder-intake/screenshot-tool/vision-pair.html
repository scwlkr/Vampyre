Screenshot Tool Vision Pair

Two Builder directions for a real macOS screenshot tool with quick markup features similar in spirit to ShareX.

Project: screenshot-tool Approval kind: builder-vision Approval key: screenshot-tool
Option A

RelayShot

Mac-native capture and sharing workflows for developers, support teams, and builders who want screenshots to move from screen to markup to destination quickly.

Best For

  • Developer and support evidence capture.
  • Configured outputs such as image, file path, Markdown link, or uploaded URL.
  • User-owned destinations instead of a mandatory vendor cloud.

First Baseline

  • Menu-bar app and global shortcuts.
  • Region, window, and full-screen capture.
  • Quick annotation, redaction, local history, and one approved upload path.
Option B

Pinmark

Fast local-first screenshot markup for Mac users who want to capture, redact, pin, annotate, and export polished screenshots without creating a cloud account.

Best For

  • Polished bug reports, docs, social images, and launch material.
  • Private local workflows with no upload credentials in the MVP.
  • Pinned reference screenshots and on-device OCR.

First Baseline

  • Menu-bar app and global shortcuts.
  • Region, window, and full-screen capture.
  • Annotation, redaction, pinning, OCR, beautified export, and local history.

Comparison

Dimension RelayShot Pinmark
Primary wedge Capture and route screenshots into useful destinations. Capture, redact, pin, and polish screenshots locally.
ShareX similarity High. Medium.
First baseline risk Higher, because uploads and link privacy need product policy. Lower, because cloud and routing are deferred.
Distribution bias Direct distribution first. Mac App Store or direct distribution.
Strongest reason to choose User-owned screenshot workflows are the clearest gap versus existing Mac tools. The product can reach a polished real baseline faster.

Recommendation

Vampyre's suggested default is RelayShot because the Raw Idea explicitly points toward ShareX, and workflow routing is the more distinct product bet. Choose Pinmark if build speed and local polish matter more for the first project.
